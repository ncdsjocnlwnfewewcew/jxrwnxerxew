Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClearTube free?

Yes, ClearTube is completely free and open-source software.

Do I need to sign in with a Google account?

No, ClearTube doesn't require any account to use most features, including subscriptions.

Is ClearTube available for iOS?

ClearTube is currently only available for Android devices.

Is using ClearTube legal?

ClearTube just provides an alternative interface to YouTube content. It doesn't bypass any security measures or download DRM-protected content.

How do I report bugs or request features?

You can open issues on our GitHub repository or join our community channels.

About ClearTube

ClearTube is a lightweight YouTube client focused on providing a clean, ad-free viewing experience while respecting your privacy. The app is built with simplicity and functionality in mind.
