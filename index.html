Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClearTube free?

Yes, ClearTube is completely free and open-source software with no hidden fees or premium versions. Unlike YouTube Premium which costs $11.99/month, you get all premium features at no cost.

Do I need to sign in with a Google account?

No, ClearTube doesn't require any Google account or YouTube login to use most features, including subscriptions. This enhances your privacy and prevents Google from tracking your viewing habits.

Is ClearTube available for iOS?

ClearTube is currently only available for Android devices. We don't have plans for an iOS version at this time due to Apple's App Store restrictions on YouTube alternative apps.

Is using ClearTube legal?

ClearTube just provides an alternative interface to YouTube content. It doesn't bypass any security measures or download DRM-protected content. It's similar to using a web browser with an ad blocker.

How do I report bugs or request features?

You can open issues on our GitHub repository or join our community channels to report bugs or request new features. We actively maintain ClearTube to ensure it works with the latest YouTube changes.

How is ClearTube different from YouTube Premium?

ClearTube offers all YouTube Premium features (ad-free experience, background play, and downloads) completely free, while YouTube Premium charges $11.99/month. ClearTube also offers better privacy protection by not requiring a Google account.

Can I watch age-restricted videos on ClearTube?

Yes, ClearTube allows you to watch age-restricted content without signing in to a Google account, which is required on the official YouTube app.

Is ClearTube a replacement for YouTube Vanced?

Yes, ClearTube is an excellent replacement for YouTube Vanced, which was discontinued. ClearTube offers similar features including ad blocking, background play, and downloads.
