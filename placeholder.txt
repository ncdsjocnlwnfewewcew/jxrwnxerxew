REQUIRED IMAGES FOR THE WEBSITE

Please add the following images to complete the website:

1. cleartube-logo.png
   - Size: 40x40 or larger (square format recommended)
   - Description: The ClearTube app logo

2. app-showcase.png
   - Size: At least 500px width, high resolution
   - Description: A mockup of the app running on a phone, showing the home screen or video player
   
3. screenshot-1.png through screenshot-4.png
   - Size: At least 250px width, consistent aspect ratio
   - Description: Screenshots of different screens in the app
     * screenshot-1.png: Home screen
     * screenshot-2.png: Video player
     * screenshot-3.png: Downloads section
     * screenshot-4.png: Settings

4. download-qr.png
   - Size: 150x150
   - Description: QR code that leads to the download page or APK

You can generate a QR code for free at:
- https://www.qr-code-generator.com/
- https://www.the-qrcode-generator.com/
- https://www.qrcode-monkey.com/
